# Imported Parts (GrabCAD)

Here are all the GrabCAD models I used in my Onshape assembly. **None of these are my own designs** they're off-the-shelf parts I downloaded only as reference, so I could model and fit my own printed parts around them. Full credit goes to the original GrabCAD creators.


## Pan mechanism

- **Lazy Susan bearing (4"):** https://grabcad.com/library/4-inch-lazy-susan-turntable-steel-ball-bearing-rotating-tray-table-1
- **NEMA 17 stepper motor (pan axis):** https://grabcad.com/library/steeper-motor-nema-17-1

## Tilt mechanism

- **NEMA 17 stepper + 10:1 planetary gearbox (tilt axis):** https://www.omc-stepperonline.com/mg-series-planetary-gearbox-gear-ratio-10-1-backlash-30-arc-min-for-nema-17-stepper-motor-mg17-g10
- **608ZZ ball bearing:** https://grabcad.com/library/608zz-bearing-cad-model-with-exploded-assembly-and-technical-drawing-1
- **8mm shaft lock collar:** https://grabcad.com/library/8mm-internal-diameter-shaft-collar-lock-collar-1

## Zoom mechanism

- **28BYJ-48 stepper motor:** https://grabcad.com/library/28byj-48-8

## Other (not GrabCAD)
- **1/4-20 tripod nut/screw (tripod connector)** — from Cults3D: https://cults3d.com/en/3d-model/gadget/1-4-20-nut-screw-for-photo-camera-mount
